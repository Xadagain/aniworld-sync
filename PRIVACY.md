# Privacy Notice

Last updated: July 20, 2026

## Summary

AniWorld List Backup processes lists only in your local Firefox profile. No data is sent to the developer or third parties.

## Data Processed

When you visit your AniWorld Watchlist or Subscriptions page, the extension reads the following visible information:

- Series title
- Series URL
- Cover image URL, when available

The extension stores this data in `browser.storage.local` within your Firefox profile. It also creates a JSON file in your local Downloads folder when requested or after visiting a supported list page.

## No Data Transmission

The extension has no servers and uses no analytics or advertising services. It does not send lists, credentials, cookies, or other personal data to the developer or third parties.

Firefox accesses AniWorld in the same way as during a normal website visit. AniWorld's privacy policy applies to its own data processing.

## Permissions

- `storage`: Stores the most recently read lists locally in your Firefox profile.
- `downloads`: Creates the local JSON backup file.
- Access to `aniworld.to` and `aniworld.cc`: Reads your Watchlist and Subscriptions when you visit those pages.

## Deletion

You can delete the JSON file from your Downloads folder at any time. Remove the extension or clear its Firefox extension data to remove the lists stored in Firefox.

## Contact

Please report questions or issues through this extension's GitHub repository.
